Scoring

Stop treating every
lead the same.

Some buyers are browsing. Some are ready to book. RealtorOS scores every lead on the way in, looking at budget match, timeline, intent and source quality. Your team's morning call list is already sorted by who's most likely to close.

  • 01Scored on capture. No manual triage, no rating spreadsheets, no “I think this one's hot”.
  • 02Duplicates merged. Same phone or email from two channels becomes one lead. Two execs don't call the same buyer.
  • 03Tags applied automatically. Project, configuration (2BHK / 3BHK / 4BHK), and budget band, all set on the way in.
  • 04Score updates with behaviour. Opened the email? Replied on WhatsApp? Visited site? The rating moves.
  • 05Lost reasons recorded. Required when you close a lead, so you can see the patterns at quarter-end.

Pipeline

Your team should sell,
not run the system.

Non‑Negotiables

Promises we keep.

Six lines we wrote into the RealtorOS spec. Non-negotiable, regardless of timeline, budget or convenience.

01 / Six
Trythat.ai works on day one.

The lead handoff and the unit-availability sync ship in version one. Not later. Bolting integrations on after launch always costs more.

02 / Six
Duplicate detection is not optional.

Without it, your lead count is inflated, your execs ring the same buyer twice, and your reports lie. It's live before launch.

03 / Six
Mobile is the default.

Sales lives on the road. So logging a call, scheduling a visit or moving a stage takes two taps on a phone.

04 / Six
Escalation rules are yours to set.

You decide how long a new lead can sit before it escalates, how many days inactive before reassignment, and who gets paged.

05 / Six
Official WhatsApp API only.

No grey-market automation. We use Interakt, WATI or AiSensy. We're not risking your number on tools that get banned.

06 / Six
Your data is yours.

Excel and CSV exports, whenever you want them. Leads, pipeline, reports, all of it. No lock-in.
