// 02 · Scoring

Hot leads first.
The rest, later.

Every lead gets a 1–10 score the second it lands — on budget match, purchase timeline, intent signals and source quality. Hot, warm, cold reclassifies as buyers respond, open emails or visit your site. Your team's day starts at #1.

  • 01Auto-scoring at capture. No manual triage, no spreadsheet ratings.
  • 02Duplicate detection. Same phone or email from multiple sources auto-merged. Two execs never call the same person.
  • 03Auto-tag project, configuration (2BHK / 3BHK / 4BHK) and budget band.
  • 04Reclassification as the buyer behaves — opened emails, replied to WhatsApp, visited site.
  • 05Lost-reason capture. Mandatory on close. Pattern analysis built in.

// non-negotiables · from the spec

The promises
we won't break.

Six instructions written into the RealtorOS spec. Six things we don't compromise on — regardless of timeline, budget or convenience.

01 / Six
Trythat.ai integration from Day 1.

The lead handoff from Trythat.ai into RealtorOS and the unit availability sync back to Trythat.ai are built into the first version. Not a later add-on. Retrofitting integrations is always more expensive — and we refuse to start in debt.

02 / Six
Duplicate detection is non-negotiable.

Without it, your lead count is inflated, your execs waste calls, and your reporting lies. Live before launch. No exceptions.

03 / Six
Mobile-first for execs.

Sales is on the road, in meetings, on site. Every critical action completable in 2 taps on mobile.

04 / Six
Escalation rules are configurable.

You set how many hours before a new lead escalates, how many days before inactive flags, and who gets the alert.

05 / Six
Official WhatsApp API only.

No grey-market automation tools. Interakt, WATI, AiSensy. Account bans are not a launch risk we accept.

06 / Six
All data is exportable.

Excel, CSV, anytime. Your leads, your pipeline, your reports. Full data ownership.
