10

Voice & Tone

Write the way an experienced builder talks. Plain English, specific nouns, no marketing fog. Confident, not loud.

Do

“Every lead from WhatsApp, ads, brokers and portals lands in one place.”

“By the time your team logs in, every lead is ranked.”

“Live in 14 days · your data exports anytime.”

Don’t

“Revolutionize your funnel with our AI-powered next-gen platform.”

“Unlock unparalleled insights through our cutting-edge solution.”

“Empower your sales journey with intelligent automation.”

Rules of thumb
  • Lead with the buyer’s reality, not a feature claim.
  • Specific nouns over abstract verbs. “WhatsApp drips,” not “smart engagement.”
  • No em-dashes for stylistic pauses. Use a period or a comma.
  • Numerals in product copy, never spelled out: 92 hot, not ninety-two hot.
  • “TryThat.ai” is the wordmark. “Trythat.ai” in body text is acceptable.
  • “for Realtors” always appears with “Realtors” in brand blue.
